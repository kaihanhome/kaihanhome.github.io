Welcome to Kai Han's Homepage

Kai Han is a professor at the School of Computing and Artificial Intelligence, Shanghai University of Finance and Economics (SUFE). Before joining SUFE, he served as a distinguished professor at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Soochow University (2022–2024), and as a tenured professor at the School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) until 2022. He earned his Ph.D. in Computer Science from USTC, and his B.Sc. from the Special Class for the Gifted Young at USTC. His research interests include machine learning, big data processing, algorithmic game theory, and social computing.
